## Distilled Learnings

1. Keep interface writing concise and scannable.
2. Keep language action-oriented and context-aware.
3. Keep icon and symbol usage semantically consistent.
4. Keep imagery in service of communication and tone.
5. Keep assistance and help discoverable but unobtrusive.
6. Keep privacy communication explicit and user-centered.
7. Keep brand expression intentional while preserving clarity.

## Application Heuristics For Personal-Site

- Keep hero copy short and specific.
- Keep CTA text verb-first and outcome-focused.
- Keep icon metaphors obvious to broad audiences.
- Keep visual storytelling aligned with portfolio goals.
- Keep legal or trust language readable and non-defensive.

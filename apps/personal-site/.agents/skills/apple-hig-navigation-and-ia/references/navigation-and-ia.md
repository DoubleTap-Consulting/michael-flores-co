## Distilled Learnings

1. Keep information architecture simple and predictable.
2. Use navigation patterns that are instantly familiar.
3. Surface primary destinations first; defer secondary paths.
4. Use search to reduce navigation friction in broader content sets.
5. Use menus for grouped commands and keyboard access patterns.
6. Ensure menu actions are meaningful and not bloated.
7. Keep onboarding lightweight and contextual.
8. Keep alignment and consistency strong across nav surfaces.

## Application Heuristics For Personal-Site

- Keep a small top-level nav and avoid deep nesting.
- Keep active/selected states obvious in all nav components.
- Keep section names concrete and audience-readable.
- Add command shortcuts only for frequent repeat actions.
- Keep mobile navigation behavior parallel to desktop concepts.

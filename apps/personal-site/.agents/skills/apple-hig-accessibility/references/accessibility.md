## Distilled Learnings

1. Build accessibility into core interaction, not as a patch.
2. Keep interfaces understandable without relying on color alone.
3. Keep controls operable across assistive technologies and keyboard flows.
4. Keep content adaptable to language and locale differences.
5. Keep motion and visual effects from reducing clarity.
6. Keep trust and privacy communication explicit.
7. Keep experiences inclusive across a wide range of user abilities.

## Application Heuristics For Personal-Site

- Keep heading structure strictly logical.
- Keep tab order aligned to reading and task order.
- Keep alt text and labels intentional and informative.
- Keep error messaging specific and recoverable.
- Keep reduced-motion and high-contrast behavior tested at component level.
